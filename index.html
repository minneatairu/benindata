This platform showcases an image dataset of Benin Bronzes compiled by Minne. All images were scanned from auction catalogs published between 1900-1965. Categories are based on my artistic goals for the Igùn project , and do not represent culturally contextual categorizations. See the books below.

This platform was designed as a visual supplement for "Missing Objects"—a 2024 commission by The Roy and Edna Disney Center CalArts Center .
